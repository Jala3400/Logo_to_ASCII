# Tips for Creating Images

The application draws with characters. They don't have the capacity to show details, but they have shape.

It is not recommended to have details smaller than one of these blocks, unless the shape matches that of a specific character.

Make vector drawings (.svg) and then convert them to image.
Draw defined edges and wide surfaces.

To facilitate design, you can use a 8x16 grid and ensure that the edges of the drawing always coincide with the edges and corners of a block.
